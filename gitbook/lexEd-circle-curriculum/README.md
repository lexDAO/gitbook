# H-shaped skills

What distinguishes a [legal engineer](https://medium.com/@esenesener/an-essay-on-legal-engineering-from-confusion-to-clarity-908c8e731df7) is H-shaped skills which combines two legs from the token-engineering skillseet with at least one from {law/ethics, governace, decision science}. Career paths are:

1. **rogue** - crypto-lawyer (specialising in transactions), forensic analysis
2. **summoner** - community ombuds(wo)man, corporate secretary, daosigner
3. **artificer** - risk assessor, corporate communications, mechanism design
